Recognition

Paul Harris
Fellows

The Paul Harris Fellow recognition is awarded to individuals who have made significant contributions to the Rotary Foundation. The Rotary Club of Lagos proudly counts numerous Paul Harris Fellows among its members — individuals whose generosity and commitment to service have helped advance Rotary's mission worldwide.
