66th President

Olutoyin Mobisola Odulate

Rotarian Olutoyin Mobisola Odulate was installed as the 66th President of the Rotary Club of Lagos on 3 July 2026 at The Metropolitan Club, Victoria Island, Lagos. Her installation marks a historic moment — she follows in the footsteps of her father, Past President Dr. Dele Odulate, who served as the club's 16th President.

A Legacy of Service: The Odulate family's connection to the Rotary Club of Lagos spans generations. Past President Dr. Dele Odulate's legacy of Service Above Self continues through his daughter as she leads the club into its 66th chapter.
